#### What is "Accuracy Dumping"

It's when you purposely miss your shots. For example, after you respawn and are going to the objective, you shoot at structures to lower your accuracy rating.

Also, don't forget about critical hit accuracy for heroes who can land headshots. You should generally keep this stat below <mark style="color:$success;">**20%**</mark>.

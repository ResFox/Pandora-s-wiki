### <mark style="color:$danger;">HOW TO AVOID BANS</mark>

Rust is a game that primarily bans manually. In community and official servers<br>

* I recommend using **Neck or Chest aimbones** to attract less attention.
* Use bind 8 to open menu, sinse some community server flags Insert key usage
* Try not to draw attention to yourself on your server, and don't pick fights more than necessary.
* Always keep a secondary Discord account in case someone on a server wants to share their screen with you. In that case, just delete the Rustcore files, empty your trash, and you're good to go.
* Don't try to be the most popular person on the server, and identify which servers you can have fun on and which ones are more dangerous.
* Community servers won't ban you from the game, only from the server. However, these servers are monitored by their administrators. On these servers, I recommend using very little wallhack range and playing fairly legitimately. Let yourself die with weak kits in PvP and dominate at the right moment.
* On official servers you can play with a lot of rage, just don't get too bad a reputation, because if a game master enters the server you could be in trouble.
* A**void looking directly at people through walls**. If you can't avoid this, disable wallhacks and play using **only radar**. Server moderators usually have tools to draw vectors in the direction you're looking.
* Increase your playtime with Idle. If you have over 2k hours, it's perfectly possible you're good at PvP. Don't make your playtime or Steam profile private.
* Try not to use Steam accounts with multiple recorded bans; this makes you more suspicious.
* If you buy an account with idle hours, try to also idle in other games so that your account appears legitimate.
* Always be suspicious of loot in strange places that don't make sense on community servers; it could be an admin's trap.
* Be suspicious of people who suddenly appear in strange places around your house, in impossible positions like flying. It could be an admin setting a trap to see if you have walls enabled. If so, disable your walls and use radar as soon as you notice them.
* Look this list to have knoledge of what anticheat checks
* Don't rush directly after the same resource. For example, if you're farming sulfur, don't run straight from one sulfur stone to another. Try to farm all the nearby materials and decipher the pattern. An admin can watch you with a free camera and notice you're running from ore to ore; a normal person doesn't have that ability.

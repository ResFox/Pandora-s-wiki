# History

Pandora's Box continued to provide support only for Overwatch 2 for around 1 year. Finally in 2023 we began to expand to provide support for different games such as Quake Champions, Halo, COD, Valorant, and practically any game where a pixelbot can be used.

<figure><img src="https://2000999588-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2Fxr02iQbBt5IsdQ3g6zQo%2Fuploads%2FW4XdH0GYWJ2YdpL0lv7W%2Fimage.png?alt=media&amp;token=a71d37ac-8611-45b8-ab0b-d1609d5865ce" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Nimrod was founded by two people: Kiroshi and Zap. Kiroshi is currently inactive. Throughout Pandora's Box's history, there have been many partnerships and friendships, from Cosmic, who helped build the second version of Skynet, later called Shodan, to Invalid, who was a great mentor and guided us in what was our first memory cheat. And now, we've brought SkynetAim and Veiltech to life, a project with ck0i and ak.Helz, where we aim to take external software to the next level.

<figure><img src="https://2000999588-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2Fxr02iQbBt5IsdQ3g6zQo%2Fuploads%2FW4XdH0GYWJ2YdpL0lv7W%2Fimage.png?alt=media&amp;token=a71d37ac-8611-45b8-ab0b-d1609d5865ce" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Pandora's Box has had its high points in its history. It was the most used pixelbot between the first and second seasons of Overwatch 2. We were the only third-party software not affected by XDefiant's bans, we were the first group to identify Javelin's API detections, and we were the first to discover telemetry detection. While not the largest company in the market, Pandora's Box has always worked hard and been at the forefront of discoveries, setting trends and ideas in the market. Since the implementation of DeepMouse, many have tried to copy us without really understanding how DeepMouse works.

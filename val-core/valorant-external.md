# 💎 Valorant External Setup

**Important: Follow these steps to minimize the risk of a ban**

1. **Move** all files and key to a USB drive or removable device.
2. **Run** the software and wait for the instruction message box, then press OK.
3. **Unplug** the USB drive immediately.
4. **Launch** the game only after the USB has been removed.

**Warning:** If you leave these files on your hard drive, the system's telemetry check may log them, which could lead to a ban. Always run the software from a USB and disconnect it before playing to stay safe.

### ValCore User Guide: Ban Prevention

As a **ValCore** user, your primary goal is to maintain account longevity. This guide provides concise, actionable information on how to avoid detection and stay under the radar.

The Golden Rule Never admit to using ESP. Even as a joke in chat or voice comms, mentioning third-party software can lead to manual reports and account flags. Silence is your best defense.

***

1. How to use ESP Discreetly The biggest giveaway for ESP users isn't the software itself, but unnatural movement and gamesense. Most users are caught because of "suspicious intent" during manual reviews.

**Stop Wall-Tracking**: <mark style="color:yellow;">Never follow an enemy's outline through walls</mark> with your crosshair. This is the most obvious red flag in spectator mode or recorded clips. \ <mark style="color:$danger;">**Consistent Corner Checking**</mark>: This is where most users fail. If you haven't checked a specific corner all game, do not suddenly clear it just because your ESP shows an enemy there. \
**The Rule of Logic**: If you wouldn't check a spot normally, don't check it now. Maintain your regular pathing and "default" clearing habits to look like a legitimate player.\
&#x20;**Avoid "Perfect" Reactions:** Occasionally allow yourself to be caught off guard. If you react perfectly to every off-angle or lurker, your behavior profile becomes statistically impossible for a natural player.

2. **Situational Awareness&#x20;**<mark style="color:$warning;">**When the game gets intense, keep your information advantage to yourself**</mark>**&#x20;to avoid manual flags:**

**Selective Prefiring:** Only prefire common angles where a high-elo player would naturally expect an opponent. Prefiring an obscure or unusual off-angle is a massive giveaway.\
&#x20;**Information Management:** Use the ESP to improve your own positioning and rotations. Avoid giving "impossible" calls to your teammates that you couldn't possibly know without visual confirmation.

> \[!IMPORTANT] Play like you don't know. The most successful ESP user is the one who uses the information to avoid dying, rather than seeking out every possible kill.

#### <mark style="color:$danger;">HOW TO EVADE BANS IN ARC RAIDERS:</mark>

1: DO NOT LOOK DIRECTLY THROUGH THE WALL       &#x20;

2: DO NOT RUN IN A STRAIGHT LINE TOWARDS AN ENEMY. ALWAYS LOOT AND PLAY NORMALLY (THE GAME CHECKS YOUR SPAWN POINT, WHERE THE ENEMY SPAWNS, AND VERIFIES TIME, DISTANCE TRAVELED, AND INTERACTIONS BEFORE ENCOUNTERING THEM)                                                 &#x20;

3: DO NOT KILL PEOPLE UNNECESSARILY. ONLY KILL THOSE WHO INEVITABLY CROSS YOUR PATH.

4: DO NOT PLAY WITH AIMBOT RAGE

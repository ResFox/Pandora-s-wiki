# ‼️ Requirements and Information

* Minimum Specifications (Windows 10)

  OS: Windows 10 x64 22H2

  CPU: Intel Core i5 with at least 4 cores or equivalent AMD Ryzen 5 (Gen i5 4500+ ryzen 5 1500+)

  GPU: Dedicated GPU with 8GB VRAM (NVIDIA 16xx series or higher, must support DX12) (GPU 1660S / rx6700xt or better)

  RAM: 16GB DDR4

  Storage: Have at least 15GB free just in case. (Skynet only takes 1GB at the most)
* Recommended Specifications (Windows 10/11)

  OS: Windows 11 22H2 - 24H2 (Insider Preview versions may not be supported)

  CPU: Intel Core i7-i9 with 8+ cores, AMD Ryzen 7 or 9 equivalent

  GPU: Dedicated GPU w/ 16GB VRAM (NVIDIA 20xx series or higher, AMD RX6500XT or higher)

  RAM: 32GB DDR5

  Storage: Have at least 15GB free just in case. (Skynet only takes 1GB at the most)

### Useful Information

1. All our software works using both DX11 and 12, ensure that you support both.
2. Shodan/Skynet uses AI vision models to scan and detect enemies, which will be covered more so in the "Understanding Models" section.
3. Our Soft only works on Windows. Mac and Linux are not supported as of this time.
4. Our Soft should not take up 100% of either your CPU or GPU. If it does, and you have a relatively decent PC, open a ticket immediately.

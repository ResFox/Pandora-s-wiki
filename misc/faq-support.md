### Q & A:

#### Definitions:

* Q: What does an "AI aimbot" mean?: A: An "AI Aimbot" means we use AI vision technology to detect enemies on your screen and aim at them for you, instead of injecting/reading/modifying game memory.
* Q: What does a "Pixelbot" mean?: A: A "Pixelbot" is a color aimbot, that detects colors on your screen and automatically moves the mouse towards those colors.
* Q: What does a "Flickbot" mean?: A: A "Flickbot" in simple terms just means that we flick your mouse to the nearest target on your screen for you, with a simple push of a button.
* Q: What is DeepMouse ? A: Is a SMM/EFI Software = Extensible Firmware Interface, so you dont need drivers or extra hardware, is the safest thing you can use.

SMM is a special execution mode in x86\_64 processor architectures, originally intended for firmware-level operations such as power management and hardware control. Unlike standard user-mode applications (ring 3), kernel-level code (ring 0), or even hypervisors (ring -1), SMM runs in ring -2, an isolated environment invisible to the operating system.

### Keys/HWID Resets:

* Q: About HWID Resets?: A: Our keys are HWID Locked. This means the moment you activate your key, that key is bound to your device. This is for security reasons. If you need to reset the HWID for your key for any reason, open a ticket and tell us.
* Q: I'm getting an error with my key!: A: Open a ticket. Remember that most of our keys are HWID locked, which means you cannot use them on a different device than the device you first activated the key on.

### Anti-Cheats/Models:

* Q: What games does SHODAN/DEEPMOUSE work on?: A: SHODAN/DEEPMOUSE works on every game. Just depends on the driver you're using within the SHODAN/DEEPMOUSE app. Here's a list of the anti-cheats we bypass: FACE-IT (requires DeepMouse/Skynet Elite private version) Vanguard EAC EOS ACE NEACProtect Ricochet Battleye
* Q: I want a new model!: A: Open a ticket with the model you want to buy. We support virtually every game, and if on the off-chance we don't, we'll let you know.

### <mark style="color:$danger;">HOW TO EVADE BANS IN TARKOV:</mark>

#### User accounts with less than 1500 hours:

1- Keep your K/D low. My recommendation is to play one real raid and then kill yourself in the next one. You dont need lose loot, you can suicide your self in night factory with just a pistol in hands.

2- Evade farm Goons, never kill the 3 Goons if you have less than 800 hours. (you dont want this achievement in your profile)

3- Do not stack the achievements for defeating bosses more than 10 times.

4- Don't kill PMCs unless you have a mission that requires it.

5- Suicide your Scav every time he loads

6- Make sure you have Scav raids (normal players play scav)

7- Evade use CHEATER loot: Like 6SH118 bag

8- Evade kill streamers if you recognice one

9- Never pick first, let the other player see you first

10- Evade abuse Snipers

11- Evade kill Gluhar with less than 500 hours in your account (this boss is hard for legit players)

12- Dont farm Killa dress

13- Evade farm Cultists if you have less than 800 hours (you dont want this achievement in your profile)

14- Avoid having short raids

15- Evade Wipe teams. If you can just kill 1 person of that team and run.&#x20;
